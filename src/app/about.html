About Email Tracker

Email Tracker was created in an attempt to engage students learning about web privacy.

Why Email Tacker?

It is hard to imagine how simply opening an email or clicking a link can reveal private information.
The idea behind Email Tracker is to let students do the tracking so they can experience it first hand. This reflects my basic teaching philosophy - Computer Science is a contact sport - learn by doing.

Email Tracker's target audience is novice programmers or non-programmers interested in understanding more about web privacy issues. The code and interface are intended to be simple enough that non-technical readers can understand how it works.
